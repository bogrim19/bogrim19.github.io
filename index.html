Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view a specific date?

Tap the "Date" tab at the bottom and use the slider to navigate to any date from 1900-2100. Swipe through centuries of lunar data at your fingertips.

What does VOC (Void of Course) mean?

The Moon is Void of Course between its last major aspect in a zodiac sign and when it enters the next sign. Many astrologers consider this period important for timing activities and decision-making—some traditions suggest avoiding major decisions or new ventures during VOC periods.

Learn more about VOC on Astro-Seek →

Does this app require internet?

No! All 200 years of astronomical data is pre-calculated and stored locally on your device. Moon Codex works perfectly offline—no internet connection needed, ever.

How accurate is the moon data?

Moon Codex uses Swiss Ephemeris calculations—the same professional-grade astronomical library used by astronomers and astrologers worldwide. Accurate to within ±1 minute for all phase transitions, sign changes, and VOC periods.

How do I add widgets to my home screen?

Long-press anywhere on your home screen → tap the "+" icon (top left) → search "Moon Codex" → select your preferred widget size → tap "Add Widget". Available in small, medium, and large sizes.

Why does the moon look different than what I see outside?

The moon's visual appearance varies based on your location, viewing angle, and atmospheric conditions. Moon Codex shows the astronomical phase, which is universal and scientifically accurate regardless of where you are on Earth.

About

Moon Codex was built by an iOS developer passionate about astronomy and astrology. It provides precise lunar tracking for astrologers, gardeners, and anyone interested in lunar cycles.
